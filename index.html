The Wheel Rules

The Wheel is a layered wheel game. You launch from the center and the round ends on the final landed multiplier.

How A Round Resolves

  1. Your bet is placed when you press Place Bet.
  2. The ball travels and collides with slice walls.
  3. Multiplier slices pay only when they are landed as the winning hit.
  4. Blue breakthrough slices (double-chevron icon) advance the ball to the next outer layer after being armed and hit again.
  5. The payout is bet × final multiplier where the round ends.

Important Notes

  • Only the final landed multiplier pays.
  • There is no chaining payout from previous layers.
  • Some slices are 0x and end the round with no return.
  • Max theoretical path in this game build is 10,000x.
